### 1. Chicago City Sticker FAQ
**Status:** Ready to write
**Slug:** `/blog/chicago-city-sticker-faq`
**Why:** High search volume across all Chicago car owners. LV2 connection: you need a city sticker to qualify for a resident permit in Zone 383.
**Key facts to include:**
- All Chicago residents who own vehicles must buy a city sticker from the City Clerk, regardless of where the vehicle is registered
- Annual renewal deadline (usually end of June). First violation: $200. Second: $400.
- City Clerk also sells guest passes for permit parking (general, citywide)
- The alderman's office cannot issue city stickers or general guest permits -- EXCEPT for LV2 Cubs Game guest passes (44th Ward specific)
- Link: City Clerk website (clerk.cityofchicago.org)
- Tie-in: city sticker is required before you can apply for a resident permit sticker
**SEO targets:** "chicago city sticker," "chicago vehicle sticker," "chicago city sticker renewal"
