LV2 PARK
When LV2 is active: 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM on all Cubs home games (day or night) and major events at Wrigley Field. Enforcement starts the moment the clock turns 5 PM. Cars without a valid LV2 permit are towed immediately — there is no warning period. To retrieve a towed vehicle: call 311 or the Cubs tow hotline at 866-4-CPD-TOW. Auto Pound #6.
