LV2 Zone Boundary Streets

The LV2 zone covers the dense residential blocks immediately surrounding Wrigley Field. The rough boundaries:

DirectionBoundary streetNotes
NorthIrving Park Road (4000 N)Northern edge of zone
SouthBelmont Avenue (3200 N)Southern edge, varies by block
WestClark Street (700 W)Western edge along Clark
EastHalsted Street (800 W)Eastern edge varies

The zone is not a perfect rectangle. Some blocks at the edges have partial coverage. The address checker above uses the official zone GeoJSON to give you a precise answer for your address.

Neighboring Permit Zones

The LV2 zone sits within a larger permit parking area. The streets just outside the LV2 boundary may fall under Zone 383 (Lakeview) or Zone 143 (Lincoln Park). Those zones operate year-round, not just on game days.

For a full comparison of how the zones overlap, see the Chicago permit zones guide.
