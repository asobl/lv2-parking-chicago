Why tickets cluster on the west

FOIA gap: entire eastern Zone 383 missing Sheffield, Kenmore, Clifton, Racine, Seminary — all shown as purple dashed lines — have zero tickets in the FOIA data. These streets are adjacent to Wrigley Field and almost certainly the most-ticketed streets in the zone. FOIA Request #2 targets these.
LV2 west strip is NOT Zone 383 — and is in the 47th Ward Marshfield (2,269 tickets), Paulina (1,323), Hermitage (901), Roscoe (851) are all WEST of Ashland — they are in the LV2 west strip zone (§9-68-023), not Zone 383 proper (§9-68-022). These streets are in the 47th Ward. Note: the official 2023 ward boundary shows the 44th Ward's western edge at Southport/Greenview (not Ashland), so the Zone 383 strip from Greenview to Ashland is also 47th Ward territory.
Commercial streets inside Zone 383 have almost no tickets Addison (4 tickets), Irving Park Rd (1 ticket), Ashland (2 tickets). Bars, restaurants, parking lots along these corridors don't have residential permit requirements.
Zone 383 E-W streets do have data — western blocks only Belle Plaine (465 tickets at 1400-1558W), Berteau (268 at 1402-1533W), Waveland (222 at 1602-1798W). The EASTERN portions of these streets (800-1400W, near Sheffield/Kenmore) have zero tickets — same FOIA gap.

Coordinate accuracy

Streets use Nominatim-verified coordinates 9 Nominatim-verified anchor streets (Belmont, Roscoe, Cornelia, Eddy, Addison, Grace, Irving Park, Montrose, Wilson — all error <6m). All others interpolated from nearest anchors. Warning: 11 raw Nominatim results were >100m wrong (returned the same street name from the wrong Chicago neighborhood) and were replaced with interpolated values. Worst offenders: Warner (1km off), Cuyler (1.35km off). Full table in internal/street-coords.json.
Grid math was off by up to 120m north The original formula (LAT_MADISON + n/100 * 0.001810) drifts. Hutchinson: grid said 41.9585 but actual is 41.9591. All grid-computed values replaced.
LV2 North lines stop at Southport (1400W), not Clark The ordinance extends LV2 North east to Clark (~900W), but Clark at these latitudes is the western boundary of Graceland Cemetery. Flat horizontal lines at Clark longitude cross the cemetery. Lines are shown only to Southport (where permit enforcement actually clusters). The zone's full east boundary is shown by the Zone Boundaries polygon toggle.
