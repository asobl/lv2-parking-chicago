## The Core Thesis

The FOIA data is the story. 9,434 tickets. Real numbers. No other site has this.

"We FOIAed 5 years of towing data from the city and built a free tool so Lakeview residents stop getting caught." That is a Block Club Chicago article. That article is a backlink. That backlink is traffic. Traffic unlocks FlexOffers and AdSense.

Everything in this plan serves that chain.

---

## Timing: Go Now, Not Next Week

Cubs games are happening now. The LV2 zone is active. The urgency narrative is at its peak.

Waiting a week loses:
- The opening-of-season angle for journalists
- The "first game day reminder" moment on community boards
- Early Reddit traction that compounds

The one exception: don't cold-pitch journalists until you've done soft-launch first (Reddit, Nextdoor). Get the site some traffic and real subscribers before you say "here's my new project." Journalists don't validate empty sites.

**Sequence: Community first (this week) -> Journalist pitch (next week, armed with early traction numbers)**

## Revenue Unlock Logic

FlexOffers rejected: traffic too low. AdSense: not applied yet. Email sponsorships: future.

What needs to happen for revenue to open up:

1. **FlexOffers approval threshold:** ~500 sessions/28-day window. GA4 monitor already set up to alert at 500.
2. **Email list target:** 200 subscribers to be credible for local sponsorships (parking garages, Wrigleyville bars). Monday digest is the retention mechanism.
3. **SpotHero button:** goes back in `renderHero()` and `buildDetailPanel()` once FlexOffers approves.

GTM goal is not just "get traffic." It's "get to 500 sessions + 200 subscribers so the monetization gates open."

## What NOT to Do

- Don't spam 10 subreddits with the same post. One good post in r/chicago is better than five thin ones.
- Don't pitch journalists with "I built a website." Pitch them with a data story: the 9,434 tickets, the street rankings, the year-over-year patterns.
- Don't beg for backlinks. Give alderman and civic orgs a genuine reason to link ("free resource for your constituents").
- Don't buy links or use SEO farms. The site is getting traffic from real people in the neighborhood -- don't compromise that with spammy signals.
